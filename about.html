About Me

I'm Kamalesh Balan, a UX designer with a strong foundation in Human-Computer Interaction and Interaction Design. My work centers on designing inclusive, empathetic, and intuitive digital experiences that solve real-world problems. I approach each project with curiosity, a sharp eye for usability, and deep respect for users' lived experiences.

Currently pursuing my graduate studies in Information Science, I combine academic rigor with practical experience across research, wireframing, prototyping, and testing. Whether it's auditing an e-commerce interface or prototyping a wearable system, I value clarity, accessibility, and emotional resonance in design.

Outside of work, you can find me sketching ideas in a notebook, exploring new research on AI and ethics, or cycling with music in my ears.

Tools & Skills

  • Design: Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch
  • Research: User interviews, usability testing, heuristic evaluations
  • Tech: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub
  • Interests: Wearable computing, ethical AI, healthcare UX
