About Me

Hi! I'm Kamalesh Balan, a UX designer and graduate student in Human-Computer Interaction at Drexel University. My academic and professional journey began in Game Design and Animation, which shaped my creative problem-solving and design thinking skills.

My work blends analytical research with visual design to create intuitive, inclusive, and engaging user experiences. I enjoy prototyping ideas, running usability tests, and iterating based on feedback. My experience spans user research, wireframing, brand design, and web development.
